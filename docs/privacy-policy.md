# PRLifts Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 2026-05-12

PRLifts ("we", "us", "our") is a fitness tracking application. This policy explains
what personal data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have over it.

---

## Data Collected

### Account Information

- Email address (optional — not required if you sign in with Apple)
- Display name
- Date of birth (optional — used for benchmarking)
- Gender (optional — used for benchmarking)
- Fitness goal (optional — used to personalise AI coaching insights)

### Workout Data

- Exercise names and types
- Weights, reps, sets, duration, distance
- Rate of Perceived Exertion (RPE)
- Workout dates and times
- Workout names and notes (optional, free-text)

### Device and Technical Information

- Device model and iOS version (for compatibility and support)
- App version
- Anonymised crash reports (via Sentry)
- Anonymous usage events identified by a random ID, not your email (via PostHog)

### Biometric Data (Future Self Feature — currently disabled for external users)

The AI image generation feature allows you to submit a photo to generate a
visualisation of a healthier version of yourself. This feature is not available
to external users while we complete a Data Processing Agreement with our image
generation provider (Fal.ai). When enabled, your original photo is permanently
deleted within 60 seconds of processing. Explicit consent is required before
any photo is processed.

---

## How We Use Your Data

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Account information | Create and maintain your account; personalise your experience |
| Workout data | Track fitness progress; detect personal records; generate AI coaching insights |
| Fitness goal, age, gender | Personalise AI feedback; benchmarking comparisons |
| Device and crash data | Fix bugs; maintain app compatibility |
| Anonymous usage events | Understand feature usage; manage feature flags |

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

Workout insights are generated by sending structured workout data (exercise names,
weights, reps, goal) to the Anthropic Claude API. Your name, email address, and
free-text notes are never sent to any AI provider.

---

## Third-Party Processors

We use the following services to operate PRLifts. Each receives only the data
necessary for its stated purpose.

### Anthropic / Claude API

**Purpose:** AI text generation for workout coaching insights.  
**Data received:** Structured workout data (exercise names, weights, reps, dates,
user goal). No name, email, or free-text content.  
**Retention:** Covered by Anthropic's API terms. Prompts are not used for model training
under the standard API agreement (verify current policy at anthropic.com/privacy).

### Supabase

**Purpose:** Primary database, authentication, and file storage.  
**Data received:** All user account and workout data; biometric consent records; sync logs.  
**DPA status:** Covered by Supabase's DPA (accepted via Supabase terms of service).  
**Transfer mechanism:** Standard Contractual Clauses for EU/US transfers.

### Railway

**Purpose:** API server hosting.  
**Data received:** Structured request logs containing no PII (user IDs are anonymised).
User photos are never written to the Railway filesystem.  
**DPA status:** Railway DPA accepted.  
**Retention:** Application logs are retained for 7 days.

### Upstash Redis

**Purpose:** Rate limiting counter store.  
**Data received:** Request counts keyed by anonymous user identifier and endpoint.
No user content is stored.  
**DPA status:** Upstash DPA accepted.  
**Retention:** Rate limit counters expire automatically within 60 seconds to 1 hour
depending on the endpoint tier.

### Fal.ai

**Purpose:** AI image generation for the Future Self feature.  
**Data received:** User photo (biometric data) and a structured generation prompt
(goal, gender) — when the feature is enabled.  
**DPA status:** ⚠️ DPA not yet signed. The Future Self feature is disabled for all
external users until the Fal.ai DPA is executed. No user photos are transmitted to
Fal.ai while the feature is disabled.  
**Intended retention when enabled:** Zero — the DPA will prohibit any retention
beyond the immediate image generation request.

## Data Retention

| Data type | Retained until |
|---|---|
| Account and workout data | Account deletion (deleted synchronously on request) |
| Personal records | Account deletion |
| AI coaching logs (prompts and responses) | 30 days (automated nightly deletion) |
| Async job records | 7 days after completion |
| Generated future self image | User deletes it, or account deletion |
| Original user photo (in transit) | Deleted within 60 seconds of processing |
| Biometric consent record | Account deletion + 1 year (legal compliance hold) |
| Anonymised crash reports (Sentry) | 90 days |
| Anonymous analytics events (PostHog) | Per PostHog plan |
| Railway application logs | 7 days |

The biometric consent record is retained for one year after account deletion as
required for compliance with applicable biometric data laws. All other personal
data is deleted synchronously when you delete your account.

---

## Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account from within the app: **Settings → Delete Account**.

Account deletion is synchronous and completes within 30 seconds. It permanently
deletes your account and all associated data, including workout history, personal
records, and any generated images. This action cannot be undone.

Deletion cascade order:

1. Workout sets
2. Workouts
3. Async jobs
4. Biometric consent record (marked deleted; retained 1 year for compliance)
5. User profile
6. Authentication identity (Supabase Auth)

After deletion, no residual data associated with your account is retained on our
servers, except the biometric consent record described above.

## User Rights

### GDPR (EU/EEA Users)

You have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Deletion** — request erasure of your personal data (available in-app via
  Settings → Delete Account, or by contacting us)
- **Portability** — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Correction** — request correction of inaccurate data
- **Objection** — object to processing where our legal basis is legitimate interest
- **Complaint** — lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond
within 30 days.

### CCPA (California Residents)

You have the right to:

- **Opt out of sale** — we do not sell your personal data. There is nothing to
  opt out of, but you may contact us to confirm this in writing.
- **Deletion** — request deletion of your personal data. Available in-app via
  Settings → Delete Account, or by contacting us.
- **Know** — request disclosure of the categories of personal data we collect,
  the purposes for which we use it, and the third parties with whom we share it.
  This policy constitutes that disclosure.

We do not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.
